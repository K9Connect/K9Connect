K9 Connect: Inception and Conception




K9 Connect started out with 4 friends getting together at the doggy park. We all had different breed of dogs and discussed how hard it was to find someone to breed with our particular canine companion. Since we were all tech-savvy we decided to do something about this and possibly help our fellow dog owners in the process.

This gave birth to the K9 Connect website. We no longer have to search out people in person at doggy parks or scower the web to find a suitable match. We can now log into K9 Connect and do that from the comfort of our home. this makes it less stressful for us and our canine companions.
